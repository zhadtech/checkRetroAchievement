# Must-Have Games

## Arcade

- **64th Street: A Detective Story** – A 1920s detective-themed beat-'em-up.

- **Boogie Wings (The Great Ragtime Show)** – A steampunk shooter where you pilot a biplane with a hook.

- **Checkered Flag** – A simple, top-down racer similar to Micro Machines.

- **Crossed Swords 2** – A behind-the-back hack-and-slash (Neo Geo MVS/CD).

- **Demon Front** – A run-and-gun game similar to Metal Slug but with magical pets.

- **Fast Lane** – A maze/driving hybrid where you collect items while avoiding a monster truck.

- **Gunbarich** – A colorful mix of Arkanoid brick-breaking and pinball.

- **In the Hunt** – A submarine shoot-'em-up with stunning pixel art by the Metal Slug team.

- **Logic Pro 2** – A frantic version of Picross with enemies and timers.

- **Magical Error wo Sagase** – A "spot the difference" game with mirrored and moving images.

- **Stone Ball** – A caveman soccer game where you club opponents.

- **Wizard Fire** – An isometric, Diablo-style multiplayer dungeon crawler.

## Atari 2600

- **Crackpots** – You play as a gardener dropping pots on bugs.

- **Demon Attack** – A fixed shooter blasting waves of colorful aliens.

- **Enduro** – A racing game known for its weather effects (fog/snow).

- **Frogs and Flies** – A cozy multiplayer game about catching bugs.

- **Haunted House** – A horror-themed adventure game.

- **Moon Sweeper** – A space shooter with asteroid fields and moon surface rescues.

- **River Raid** – A vertical scrolling jet shooter requiring fuel management.

- **Seaquest** – An underwater rescue game defending against sharks and subs.

## Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

- **Arkista's Ring** – An action-adventure game with puzzle elements.

- **Deja Vu** – A point-and-click adventure game set in 1940s Chicago.

- **Darkwing Duck** – A platformer based on the Disney animated series.

- **Jurassic Park** – An action-adventure game based on the film (NES version).

- **Legacy of the Wizard** – An action RPG with multiple playable characters.

- **Pin-Bot** – A faithful 8-bit recreation of the Williams pinball table.

- **Shadowgate** – A deadly first-person point-and-click adventure.

- **Shadow of the Ninja** – An action platformer with co-op and weapon upgrades.

- **Sweet Home** – The RPG precursor to Resident Evil set in a haunted mansion.

- **Uninvited** – A horror-themed point-and-click adventure game.

## Super Nintendo (SNES)

- **Battle Pinball** – A Japanese pinball game featuring Ultraman and Gundam.

- **Clock Tower** – A tense point-and-click survival horror game featuring "Scissorman."

- **Dragon View** – An action RPG mixing first-person exploration with side-scrolling combat.

- **E.V.O.: Search for Eden** – An action RPG where you evolve your creature through history.

- **Ghoul Patrol** – The sequel to Zombies Ate My Neighbors.

- **The King of Dragons** – A class-based fantasy beat-'em-up by Capcom with RPG leveling.

- **Magical Pop'n** – A bright action-platformer with a grappling hook mechanic.

- **Maui Mallard in Cold Shadow** – A platformer starring Donald Duck as a ninja.

- **The Ninja Warriors** – A side-scrolling beat-'em-up with cyborg ninjas.

- **Nosferatu** – A horror-themed action game.

- **Pieces** – A competitive jigsaw puzzle game with power-ups.

- **Pinball Fantasies** – A polished pinball game with four distinct tables.

- **Space Mega Force** – A vertical shooter with destructible environments.

- **Super Castlevania 4** – A classic spooky platformer with multidirectional whipping.

- **Super Famicom Wars** – The turn-based strategy predecessor to Advance Wars.

- **Trials of Mana** – An action RPG with multiple story paths.

- **Uniracers** – A fast-paced unicycle racing game.

- **Wild Guns** – A steampunk Wild West gallery shooter.

- **Zombies Ate My Neighbors** – A run-and-gun game with horror movie references.

## Nintendo 64

- **Body Harvest** – An open-world action game involving aliens and time travel.

## Commodore 64

- **Ultima IV: Quest of the Avatar** – A classic RPG focusing on virtue and character development.

## Game Boy (Original)

- **BurgerTime Deluxe** – A puzzle-platformer where you assemble burgers.

- **Castle Quest** – An action-adventure game.

- **Gargoyle Quest** – An action-platformer with RPG elements.

- **Kid Dracula** – A spooky platformer featuring a child vampire.

- **Legend of the River King** – A fishing RPG with adventure elements.

- **Monster Max** – A puzzle-adventure game.

- **Revenge of the Gator** – A pinball game.

- **Trip World** – A colorful platformer with transformation abilities.

## Game Boy Color

- **Alone in the Dark** – A survival horror game port.

- **Cannon Fodder** – A strategy-action game.

- **Great Greed** – An RPG with a food-themed world.

- **Heroes of Might and Magic II** – A strategy game port.

- **Pinball** – A pinball game.

- **Scooby-Doo: Classic Creep Capers** – A puzzle-adventure game based on the cartoon.

- **Spy vs. Spy** – A strategy game based on the MAD Magazine characters.

- **The Getaway: High Speed II** – A handheld port of the driving-themed pinball table.

- **Microsoft Pinball Arcade** – A GBC collection of real Gottlieb tables.

- **QIX** – A puzzle game where you draw lines to capture territory.

- **Roland's Curse 2** – A black-and-white handheld action RPG.

- **Street Fighter Alpha: Warriors' Dreams** – An impressive fighting game port for GBC.

## Game Boy Advance (GBA)

- **Bit Generations Series** – Minimalist games including Dotstream, Coloris, and Orbital.

- **Bookworm** – An addictive word-forming puzzle game.

- **Dexter's Laboratory: Chess Challenge** – A Battle Chess style game with cartoons.

- **Droopy's Tennis Open** – A wacky arcade tennis game with special courts.

- **Elf Bowling 1 & 2** – Infamous shovelware where Santa bowls with elves.

- **Hamtaro: Ham-Ham Heartbreak** – A cozy, clever adventure/puzzle game.

- **Invader** – A space shooter game.

- **It's Mr. Pants** – A bizarre puzzle game by Rare with a scribbly art style.

- **Juka and the Monophonic Menace** – An action-adventure game.

- **Kong: The 8th Wonder of the World** – A top-down puzzle-adventure based on the movie.

- **Kuru Kuru Kururin** – A skill game navigating a spinning stick through mazes.

- **Lilo & Stitch** – A surprisingly hard Contra-style shooter.

- **Mazes of Fate** – A first-person dungeon crawler RPG.

- **Nancy Drew: Message in a Haunted Mansion** – A point-and-click mystery.

- **Pac-Man Pinball Advance** – A pinball game where Pac-Man eats pellets on the table.

- **Pokémon Pinball: Ruby & Sapphire** – A pinball game with monster-catching mechanics.

- **Rhythm Tengoku** – The Japan-only first entry in the Rhythm Heaven series.

- **Scourge: Hive** – A top-down action game.

- **Sea Trader: Rise of Taipan** – A trading and strategy game.

- **The Simpsons: Road Rage** – A Crazy Taxi clone set in Springfield.

- **Steel Empire** – A steampunk shoot-'em-up port.

- **The Pinball of the Dead** – A pinball game with zombie theme.

- **TMNT** – A Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles game.

- **Tomato Adventure** – A quirky RPG by the Mario & Luigi developers.

- **WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Microgames!** – A collection of chaotic 5-second microgames.

## Nintendo 3DS

- **Dragon Quest VII** – A massive RPG (the 3DS remake fixes pacing issues).

## Sega Master System

- **Blades of Vengeance** – An action-platformer.

- **Cloud Master** – A shooter where you fly on a cloud fighting weird enemies.

- **Dragon's Revenge** – A pinball game.

- **Gangster Town** – A light gun shooter with a 1920s mobster theme.

- **Ghouls 'n Ghosts** – A challenging action-platformer.

- **Haunting Starring Polterguy** – A horror-themed action game.

- **Kenseiden** – A methodical samurai action-platformer.

- **Master of Darkness** – A Castlevania-style vampire hunter game.

- **Montezuma's Revenge** – A screen-by-screen puzzle platformer.

- **The Ooze** – An action game with a blob protagonist.

- **Urban Strike** – A helicopter combat game.

- **Zero Tolerance** – A first-person shooter.

## Sega Genesis / Mega Drive

- **Empire of Steel** – A steampunk shooter with "silent storytelling."

- **Pirates Gold** – A strategy-adventure game about piracy.

- **Psycho Pinball** – A colorful pinball game with four themed tables.

- **Scooby-Doo and Mystery of Blake's Hotel** – A puzzle-adventure game.

- **Shadowrun** – A cyberpunk RPG based on the tabletop game.

- **Sonic Spinball** – A platformer/pinball hybrid set in the Sonic universe.

- **Splatterhouse 3** – A gory beat-'em-up horror game.

## Sega Game Gear

- **Ayrton Senna's Super Monaco GP II** – A solid handheld racing game.

- **Battletoads** – A faithful, difficult port of the beat-'em-up.

- **Dragon Crystal** – A roguelike with randomized levels and mystery potions.

- **Mega Man** – An original Mega Man title for the handheld.

- **Shinobi II: The Silent Fury** – An action game with Metroidvania elements.

- **Slider** – A puzzle game about changing tile colors.

- **Sonic Drift 2** – A kart racer with Sonic characters.

- **Tails Adventure** – A slower-paced Metroidvania starring Tails using bombs.

## PlayStation 1 (PS1)

- **Apocalypse** – A run-and-gun shooter starring Bruce Willis.

- **Brave Fencer Musashi** – An action RPG with a day/night cycle and skill stealing.

- **Deception III: Dark Delusion** – A strategy game about setting traps for enemies.

- **Hogs of War** – A 3D turn-based strategy game (like Worms) with pigs.

- **The Misadventures of Tron Bonne** – A genre-blending spin-off of Mega Man Legends.

- **Resident Evil 2** – The classic survival horror game.

- **Tenchu: Stealth Assassins** – A stealth-action game featuring ninjas.

- **Thrill Kill** – An unreleased, violent 4-player arena fighting game.

- **Tomb Raider** – An action-adventure game featuring Lara Croft.

## PlayStation 2 (PS2)

- **Katamari Damacy** – A quirky game about rolling objects into a giant ball.

## TurboGrafx-16 / PC Engine

- **Devil's Crush** – A gothic, occult-themed pinball game.

- **Final Lap Twin** – A racing game with a full RPG story mode.

## Neo Geo Pocket Color

- **SNK vs. Capcom: The Match of the Millennium** – A highly regarded handheld fighter.

## PC (DOS/Windows)

- **Alone in the Dark** – The grandfather of 3D survival horror.

- **Capture the Flag** – A turn-based strategy game simulating the outdoor game.
